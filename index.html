🏛️ HQ & Strong Base Growth

Your HQ is your base’s core. Keep key buildings equal to your HQ for max strength.

MUST Match Your HQ

These directly boost power, durability, and troop strength:

  • Troop Camps – Unlock/promote highest troops (promote first, train second).
  • Military Center – Bigger troop cap = stronger marches.
  • Hospitals – Fewer troop deaths in war.
  • City Walls – More HQ health/durability (can fall slightly behind).
  • Alliance Center – Needed for HQ upgrades + higher help cap.
  • Laboratory – Research = stronger troops, heroes, HQ.
  • Rally Center – More rally slots for stronger attacks.

Can Lag Behind (Upgrade When Convenient)

  • Toxic Garden / Dawn Tower / Steel Factory – Extra formation power + troop cap.
  • Restaurant – Faster troop training.
  • Villa – Faster construction.
  • Warehouse – Protects resources from being looted (crucial once SvS starts).
  • Production Center – Higher troop load = more loot per trip, less fuel, faster tasks.

Quick Summary: Match HQ: Camps, Military Center, Hospitals, Walls, Alliance Center, Lab, Rally Center. Let lag: Toxic Garden, Dawn Tower, Steel Factory, Restaurant, Villa.

🔬 Research & Laboratory Order

Research is the backbone of progress. Done right, it boosts power, speeds, and event performance.

Badge‑Free First

Max out badge‑free research early. It’s pure value:

  • Shelter Building – Huge speed boosts, no badges.
  • Elite Troops – Universal training buffs.
  • Allied Troops – Modest stat gains, good secondary focus.
  • HQ Management – Shortens upgrade times, ideal for two‑lab setups.

Badge Priorities & Lab Order

Save badges for high‑impact tech and time them with events:

  • Alliance Recognition → up to Event King 10 (unlocks Super Rewards).
  • Elite Troops → 100% → unlocks Military Strategies.
  • Military Strategies → 45% → unlocks Peace Shield → Urgent Rescue Lv. 10.
  • Hero Training → 100% (max).
  • Military Strategies → 100% (late game).
  • Siege to Seize & Fully Armed Alliance last, unless you’re spending heavily on Police Badges.

Two‑Lab Strategy: Lab 1 runs badge‑free tech nonstop. Lab 2 handles badge‑heavy tech timed with events.

🧑‍🌾 Refugees & Economic Power

Refugees don’t fight — they make your base stronger behind the scenes.

What Refugees Do

Each refugee boosts a specific building or function:

  • Butler → Construction speed (Villa).
  • Community Manager → Zents production (Residences).
  • Farmer → Food production (Farmhouse).
  • Lumberjack → Wood production (Lumberyard).
  • Mechanic → Electricity production (Wind Turbine).
  • Assault Instructor → Training speed (Assaulter Camp).
  • Diplomat → Alliance help cap (Alliance Center).

Star Priority & Goals

  • 1) Butler — fastest HQ and building progression.
  • 2) Community Manager — solves the biggest mid‑game bottleneck (Zents).
  • 3) Farmer & Lumberjack — fuel upgrades, research, troop training.
  • 4) Mechanic — important later for high‑level buildings.
  • 5) Assault Instructor — good for troop power events.
  • 6) Diplomat — lowest impact unless your alliance is extremely active.

First Goal: Get Butler and Community Manager to 3★. 2★ is noticeable, 3★ is strong, 4–5★ is long‑term competitive.

🪖 Troops, March Size & Survival

Your troop strength, march size, and survival rate depend on three buildings staying equal to your HQ.

Training Camps – Troop Power Engine

Purpose: Unlock and promote higher‑tier troops.

  • Higher camp level = higher troop tier unlocked.
  • Promoting troops gives more power than training new ones.
  • Stronger troops = stronger rallies, defenses, and event performance.

Rule: Promote first, train second.

Hospitals – Troop Protection System

Purpose: Reduce troop deaths and keep your army alive.

  • Higher hospital level = more wounded capacity.
  • More wounded = fewer dead troops.
  • Fewer dead troops = faster recovery and less resource loss.

Rule: Hospitals should NEVER lag behind your HQ.

Military Center – March Size Booster

Purpose: Increase your maximum troop capacity per march.

  • Bigger marches = stronger attacks and defenses.
  • More troops = higher rally contribution.
  • Essential for capital fights, SvS, and turret defense.

Rule: Always upgrade the Military Center before pushing HQ.

🔥 Troop Quick Summary

Keep these equal to your HQ at all times:

  • Training Camps
  • Hospitals
  • Military Center

If these fall behind, your troops fall behind.

⚙️ Equipment & Cores

Evenly distributing cores gives the best stat efficiency. Weapons can sit slightly ahead, but the rest should stay even.

Even Star Levels

Equipment uses Promotion Cores to increase star levels on:

  • Gun – highest attack scaling.
  • Helmet – attack + defense.
  • Heavy Armor – defense + troop HP.
  • Power Boots – mixed stats.

Bring all four to the same star level first: ★1 → ★2 → ★3 → etc.

Gun Exception & Milestones

  • Gun can stay +1 star above the rest, but never +2 or +3.
  • Early: push all gear to ★3 evenly.
  • Mid: aim for ★5 across all gear (Gun can be ★6 for attackers).
  • Late: attackers keep Gun slightly ahead; defenders lean into Helmet/Armor.

Avoid: One high‑star piece with the others 2+ stars behind. It cripples your total stats.

🦸 Hero Factions – Season 1 Snapshot

Build around one main carry and a 3‑hero faction core for each team.

Blood Rose – Burst DPS Faction

Troop Type: Ranged DPS • Strength: High burst damage, easy fragments.

  • Katrina – Main carry. Max Skill 1 first, then Skill 2 → Skill 3, stars after.
  • Best trio: Katrina + Vivian + Selena.
  • Run 3 Blood Rose heroes for faction buff; tankiest front, Katrina backline.

Wings of Dawn – Balanced & F2P Friendly

Balanced, reliable, and extremely F2P‑friendly. Strong sustain, counters Blood Rose burst.

  • Scarlett – Main DPS. Skill 1 → Skill 2 → stars → ATK gear.
  • Core trio: Scarlett + Laura + Amelia (or Fiona).
  • Formation: Fiona front, Scarlett protected backline with Laura/Amelia.

Guard of Order – Defensive Control Core

Defensive, control‑heavy, slow but unstoppable in long fights.

  • Evelyn & Athena – Main tanks. Prioritize Skill 1, then stars.
  • Mia – Support controller with stuns, slows, and damage reduction.
  • Core: Evelyn + Athena + Mia + 2 off‑faction DPS (e.g., Blood Rose or Wings).
